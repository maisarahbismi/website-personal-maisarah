୨⎯ ACHIEVEMENT ⎯୧

It's a pleasure to get study in UiTM Rembau. Just like other students, my target was to get a Vice Chancellor Award(ANC). I managed to get a dean list for the first semester but unfortunately, I fail to do so for second semester as it was a tough time for me. However, I learned from the failure and managed to get a dean list for next semester. I'm so grateful to be able get this award as it has become my motivation to do better in future. This is during the dean award ceremony in semester 3. I'm so happy and grateful for this oppurtunity. Shoutout to my faculty club who organized this event!💖

My classmates and I went to the dean award ceremony together. Beauty with brain they said hehe💘

Alhamdulillah, I managed to get another dean award for semester 4. Words couldn't describe how grateful I am when I get this award as my semester 4 is like climbing a steep hill but it was worth it at the end of the day. Thanks to my family and friends who always help me to overcome this hard situation. Much love for them.💖

One of my biggest achievement for this year is I get to be one of the representatives of UiTM Rembau along with my friends for Pertandingan Seni Warisan Randai held at Jabatan Penyiaran Negeri Sembilan. The competition was held on 27 May 2023 and it is also aired on TV2. Our team was so excited as well as nervous because this is our first time to be in such a big competition.💫

Our team won the fifth place in the competition and considering this is our first time to perform randai, it is still a big achievement to our team. It is an eye opening experience and a great oppurtunity as a performer as all of us gained new knowledge about Negeri Sembilan's tradition. Big thanks for RAGENDA club for this oppurtunity.🥰
